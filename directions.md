CHIN 0410 Midterm Project
Spring 2026
A. Objectives
By conducting the CHIN-0410 midterm project, students will be able to showcase the Chinese
linguistic and cultural knowledge they have learned and the language skills they have gained so far.
This midterm project provides an opportunity for students to integrate their knowledge and skills of
the Chinese language both inside and outside the classroom.
B. Scenarios and Tasks
You are working at Penn’s International Students and Scholar Services, and your supervisor has
asked you to create a “Survival Guide for Studying Abroad at Penn (宾大留学指南)” for
Chinese international students. The goal is to help incoming students better prepare for their arrival
in the U.S. and adjust to college life at Penn.
Your team will create either:
• A website that presents information in an engaging and organized manner
or
• A video that introduces and explains key aspects of studying at Penn.
Your project should cover topics including (but not limited to):
1. Arriving at Philadelphia International Airport and an introduction to the city of
Philadelphia.
2. Living on Penn’s campus, including housing, dining, and student resources.
3. A typical U.S. college student’s daily life, focusing on academics, extracurricular
activities, and social life.
4. Getting around Philadelphia, including mailing services and transportation options.
C. Format
This is a team project, and you will be working with another classmate to create the website or
video. You must choose your team partner(s) by Monday, March 2.
2
Website Option
If you choose to create a website, you are free to use any website builder that you and your partner
find easy to navigate. We recommend the following free website builders:
• Wix (www.wix.com) – User-friendly with customizable templates.
• Weebly (www.weebly.com) – Simple drag-and-drop design.
Your website should include:
✔ Well-organized sections and clear navigation.
✔ Text, images, and multimedia elements to enhance engagement.
✔ Proper use of the Chinese language throughout the content.
Video Option
If you choose to create a video, your team will produce an engaging visual guide. The video can
be:
• A documentary-style video with narration and informative segments.
• A skit or role-play showing real-life scenarios in Chinese.
• A vlog-style tour of campus and Philadelphia with explanations.
Recommended tools for video creation:
• iMovie (Mac/iOS) or Windows Video Editor – Simple editing software.
• CapCut – Free mobile-friendly video editing app.
• Adobe Premiere Rush – More advanced, but user-friendly for beginners.
Your video should include:
✔ At least 12-15 minutes of content.
✔ Clear Chinese narration/dialogue (subtitles in Chinese and English).
✔ Well-structured presentation with smooth transitions and quality visuals.
✔ Proper team collaboration with all members contributing.
